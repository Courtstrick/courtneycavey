Approach

How I find & scale growth.

A repeatable operating system for finding new growth and scaling what works — not a bet on any single channel.

01 · Foundation first

Fix the engine before adding fuel

A high-performing website, technical SEO, conversion-rate optimization, and answer-engine optimization (AEO) come first. Get the core converting and citable before pouring spend into the top of the funnel.

02 · Find new channels

New channels, run like experiments

Creator programs, community, events, ABX — tested methodically with the Bullseye framework (~$3–10K per channel test) to find what actually compounds, instead of betting everything on one bet.

03 · Engine + turbo

A steady engine with strategic boosts

Adapting Lenny Rachitsky's "race car" model — a dependable growth engine that keeps compounding, plus turbo boosts layered on where the data says they'll pay off.

04 · Data & cadence

Pragmatic data, tight operating rhythm

Start from a GA4 baseline and build from whatever exists — comfortable operating with imperfect data — in a weekly/bi-weekly cadence with sales, product, and engineering.

Range note: I've built marketing from zero (founding the function at Lineup.ai) and scaled it into eight figures (Hubstaff) — so whether the job is finding the next growth channel or building the system to scale the last one, I've done both.
